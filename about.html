About Zenthos Insights

Documenting the journey of learning to code, one article at a time.

The Story

Zenthos Insights started as a personal project to document my journey into web development. What began as simple notes to myself has grown into a platform where I share tutorials, insights, and lessons learned with fellow developers around the world.

I believe that the best way to learn is to teach. By writing about the concepts I'm learning, I reinforce my own understanding while hopefully helping others who are on similar paths. Every article on this blog comes from real experience and genuine curiosity.

What You'll Find Here

Developer Journey

Personal stories and reflections on learning to code, the challenges faced, and the victories celebrated along the way.

Coding Tutorials

Practical guides and tutorials on JavaScript, web development, and modern programming practices.

Project Insights

Behind-the-scenes looks at projects I've built, including the problems solved and lessons learned.

Tech Thoughts

Perspectives on technology trends, tools, and the evolving landscape of software development.

My Philosophy

I believe that anyone can learn to code with the right resources and persistence. Technology should be accessible, and knowledge should be shared freely. This blog is my contribution to the developer community that has given me so much.

Every expert was once a beginner. Every complex application started with a single line of code. If you're just starting out, know that the struggle is worth it. Keep learning, keep building, and never stop being curious.
